# RBI Assistant & Grade B – Mains Syllabus (with sub-topics)

*RBI Assistant Mains: General Awareness, Computer Knowledge. RBI Grade B Phase 2: Economic & Social Issues, English Writing, Finance & Management. References: Banking/GA books, Testbook/guidely syllabus, RBI publications (Annual Report, Monetary Policy, Trend and Progress of Banking), Economic Survey, World Development Report.*

---

## RBI Assistant General Awareness Syllabus

| # | Topic | Sub-topics |
|---|--------|------------|
| 1 | History, Geography of India | Ancient/medieval/modern history; physical geography, states, rivers, climate; freedom movement; post-independence |
| 2 | Socio-economic policy development | Planning, NITI Aayog, poverty alleviation, employment, sustainable development, welfare schemes |
| 3 | Indian Economy | GDP, growth, sectors (agriculture, industry, services), inflation (WPI, CPI), fiscal and monetary policy, budget basics |
| 4 | State, countries, institutions | Capitals, currencies, international organisations (UN, IMF, World Bank, WTO); national institutions (RBI, SEBI, ministries) |
| 5 | Science | Basic physics, chemistry, biology; inventions; space; environment and ecology |
| 6 | Current affairs and Politics in India | National/state politics, elections, bills, ordinances, constitutional amendments; last 6–12 months news |
| 7 | Quantitative aptitude | *(Listed in GA syllabus; same as Prelims Numerical – see Prelims_syllabus.md)* |
| 8 | Human Right issues | Fundamental rights, DPSP, key judgements, international conventions |
| 9 | Logical Reasoning | *(Same as Prelims Reasoning – see Prelims_syllabus.md)* |
| 10 | Books and Authors | Famous books, awards (e.g. Booker, Jnanpith), author–book matching |

**Banking & Financial Awareness (RBI Assistant focus):** Banking awareness, financial awareness, bank management, money and banking, infrastructure and project finance, management control system, RBI functions, monetary policy, recent banking sector developments.

**Static & Misc:** Currencies and capitals, sports abbreviations, government schemes and policies, agriculture and rural development.

## RBI Assistant Computer Knowledge Syllabus

| # | Topic | Sub-topics |
|---|--------|------------|

| 3 | Devices | Peripherals: keyboard, mouse, monitor, printer, scanner; storage devices; ports and connectors |

| 8 | Internet | Web, browser, URL, HTTP/HTTPS; email; search engines; cloud basics; netiquette and security |

## RBI Grade B Phase 2 – English (Writing Skills)

Descriptive: Precis writing, Reading comprehension (passage-based), Essay on economic/social/general topics. No sub-topic list; practice from any RBI Grade B descriptive material.

---

## RBI Grade B Phase 2 – Finance and Management

*References: Standard Finance & Management books for RBI Grade B.*

**Finance:** Financial system (RBI functions, monetary policy, banking system); financial institutions (SIDBI, EXIM, NABARD, NHB); financial markets (money, forex, bond, equity; primary and secondary); fintech; inflation (WPI, CPI); Union Budget; direct/indirect taxes; GST; fiscal policy; corporate governance; financial inclusion; derivatives; risk management.

**Management:** Fundamentals of management and organisational behaviour; evolution of management thought (scientific, administrative, human relations, systems approach); personality; motivation theories (Maslow, Herzberg, Adams, Vroom); leadership; communication; conflict and negotiation.
